ERF Function

\[\begin{aligned} \text{ERF} = \frac{1}{N_{EST}} \sum_{k} \frac{1}{N_k} \sum_{i} \left( \frac{C_i^{(k)} - P_i^{(k)}}{P_i^{(k)}} \right)^2 \end{aligned} \]
  • $P^{(k)}$ is the value of the estimator $k$ for the PRIOR
  • $C^{(k)}$ is the value of the estimator $k$ for the COMPRESSED (either drawn from the PRIOR or ENHANCED)
